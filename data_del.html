Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: September 29, 2025

At Data Analyst, we respect your right to privacy and give you control over your personal data. If you would like your personal information to be deleted from our systems, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to arnoldki@pichsafe.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the body of the email, include the following information:
    • Your full name.
    • The email address or account associated with Data Analyst.
    • A brief statement requesting deletion of your data.

What Happens Next

  • Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity to ensure security.
  • After verification, we will permanently delete your data from our records within 7 business days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
